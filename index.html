Micro-Tool Candidates

One industry. One annoying workflow. One simple web tool. Try many, kill fast.

The model: Simple web app. User uploads/inputs something specific to their trade. AI processes it. They get back the tedious thing they used to spend hours on. Charge $49-199/mo. Build each in 1-2 weeks. Run ads targeting the exact job title. If it doesn't convert in 30 days, kill it and try the next one.
Source data: 318 US business types, 40 dimensions. Filtered for owner-operator accessible, low compliance, high manual pain, market size >= 8,000 businesses. Then hand-picked for workflows where the input/output is clear and AI genuinely does it better than a human spending hours.

Strategy: Ship 3-4 of these over 6-8 weeks. Run $500 in ads for each. The one that gets trial signups wins more investment. The rest get archived. Don't fall in love with any of them before the market says yes.
