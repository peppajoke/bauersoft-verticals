10 Alternative Verticals

Opportunity areas outside trades/home services. Scored from 318 US business types across 40 dimensions. Click any card for the deep dive.

Methodology

Dataset: 318 US small business types scored 1-5 across 40 dimensions including TAM, AI advantage, manual process pain, digital maturity gap, software market fragmentation, willingness to pay, defensibility, and niche potential.

These 10 were selected by composite score (weighted toward AI advantage, pain, gap, and defensibility) excluding trades/construction and home services (already analyzed separately). Diversity of category was also weighted to avoid healthcare domination.

Source data: BauerSoft market research, May 2026. All figures are estimates for the US market.
