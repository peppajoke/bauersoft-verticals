B2C Micro-Tool Candidates

Consumer-facing AI tools. Higher volume, lower price, viral potential. Things people share with friends.

← Idea Tracker B2B Candidates
B2C model: Free tier with upgrade ($5-15/mo or $3-10 per use). Distribution via social/viral/SEO, not ads. The tool needs to produce something shareable or save enough time/money that word-of-mouth carries it. Build in days, not weeks. If it doesn't get organic traction in 2 weeks, move on.
B2C vs B2B tradeoff: B2C has higher volume potential and viral upside, but lower revenue per user and less predictable retention. The best B2C micro-tools either (1) save people real money (bill negotiation, subscription audit), (2) save hours of tedious work (trip planning, event prep), or (3) produce something shareable that markets itself (highlight reels, styled photos). Ideally all three.

Pricing philosophy: Freemium or pay-per-use beats monthly subscription for most consumer tools. People will pay $5 once for something they need right now more readily than $10/mo for something they might need later.
